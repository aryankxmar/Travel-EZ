About Auroville Beach

Auroville Beach is a serene and picturesque beach located near the Auroville township in Tamil Nadu, India. It offers visitors a tranquil escape with its golden sands, azure waters, and lush greenery.

Main Attractions

  • Scenic Beauty: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Bay of Bengal and the surrounding lush landscape.
  • Water Sports: Engage in various water sports activities such as swimming, surfing, and beach volleyball.
  • Sunset Views: Witness stunning sunset views over the horizon, creating a mesmerizing ambiance.

Local Cuisine

Relish the flavors of Tamil Nadu cuisine during your visit to Auroville Beach:

  • Seafood Delicacies: Savor fresh seafood dishes such as fish curry, prawn fry, and crab masala.
  • South Indian Staples: Enjoy traditional South Indian dishes like dosa, idli, and sambar vada.
  • Coconut-based Treats: Indulge in desserts made from coconut, such as coconut laddoos and coconut payasam.

Accommodation Options

Experience comfortable stays near Auroville Beach:

  • The Promenade Hotel: A luxury beachfront hotel offering panoramic views of the Bay of Bengal and modern amenities.
  • Le Pondy: A boutique beach resort nestled amidst lush greenery, providing a serene retreat for travelers.
  • Guesthouses and Cottages: Explore budget-friendly accommodation options available in the Auroville township.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Auroville, check-in to hotel

Day 2: Relaxing day at Auroville Beach, indulge in water sports activities

Day 3: Explore Auroville township, visit Matrimandir and other attractions

Day 4: Visit nearby attractions like Pondicherry Beach and Auroville Bakery

Day 5: Leisure day for shopping and exploring local markets

Day 6: Departure from Auroville
