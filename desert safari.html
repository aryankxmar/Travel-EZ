Jaisalmer Desert Safari

About the Safari

Experience the magic of the Thar Desert with our Jaisalmer Desert Safari. Located in the heart of Rajasthan, Jaisalmer offers a unique opportunity to explore the vast desert landscape, immerse yourself in local culture, and witness breathtaking sunsets over the sand dunes.

Our safari packages include thrilling camel rides, traditional Rajasthani folk performances, overnight camping in luxury tents, and delicious local cuisine served under the starlit sky.

Join us for an unforgettable adventure into the enchanting world of the desert.

Safari Highlights

  • Camel Rides: Explore the desert landscape on camelback, the traditional mode of transportation in the Thar Desert.
  • Cultural Performances: Enjoy vibrant folk dances, music performances, and puppet shows showcasing the rich heritage of Rajasthan.
  • Overnight Camping: Experience the tranquility of the desert night as you camp in luxury tents amidst the sand dunes, complete with modern amenities.
  • Sunset Views: Witness mesmerizing sunsets over the desert horizon, painting the sky with hues of orange and gold.
  • Local Cuisine: Indulge in authentic Rajasthani cuisine, including dal bati churma, gatte ki sabzi, and more, prepared by local chefs.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Jaisalmer, check-in to the desert camp

Day 2: Camel safari to explore the desert, cultural performances in the evening

Day 3: Sunrise experience, breakfast at the camp, departure from Jaisalmer
