About Radhanagar Beach

Radhanagar Beach, also known as Beach No. 7, is located on Havelock Island in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands of India. Renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, soft white sands, and crystal-clear turquoise waters, it is considered one of the best beaches in Asia.

Main Attractions

  • Scenic Beauty: Enjoy the picturesque landscape of Radhanagar Beach, surrounded by lush greenery and swaying palm trees.
  • Swimming and Snorkeling: Take a dip in the tranquil waters or explore the vibrant marine life through snorkeling activities.
  • Sunset Views: Witness spectacular sunsets over the horizon, casting hues of orange and pink across the sky.

Local Cuisine

Indulge in the flavors of Andamanese cuisine during your visit to Radhanagar Beach:

  • Seafood Delicacies: Feast on fresh seafood dishes such as grilled fish, lobster curry, and crab masala prepared with local spices.
  • Coconut-based Treats: Try coconut-based sweets like coconut laddoos and coconut barfi for a taste of traditional Andamanese desserts.
  • Tropical Fruits: Savor tropical fruits like mangoes, bananas, and papayas, grown abundantly in the fertile soil of the Andaman Islands.

Accommodation Options

Experience comfortable stays near Radhanagar Beach:

  • Beach Resorts: Choose from a selection of luxurious beach resorts offering stunning ocean views and modern amenities.
  • Eco-friendly Cottages: Stay in eco-friendly cottages nestled amidst nature, providing a peaceful and sustainable accommodation option.
  • Guesthouses and Homestays: Opt for budget-friendly guesthouses and homestays for a cozy and authentic island experience.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair, transfer to Havelock Island via ferry

Day 2: Relaxing day at Radhanagar Beach, sunbathing and swimming

Day 3: Snorkeling excursion to explore the vibrant coral reefs

Day 4: Visit Elephant Beach for water sports activities like banana boat rides and jet skiing

Day 5: Leisure day for shopping and exploring local markets

Day 6: Departure from Havelock Island, return to Port Blair
