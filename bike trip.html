Manali to Leh Bike Trip

About the Trip

The Manali to Leh bike trip is a legendary adventure through the stunning landscapes of the Indian Himalayas. Covering a distance of approximately 470 kilometers, the journey takes you through some of the highest motorable passes in the world, including the famous Khardung La Pass.

Starting from Manali, Himachal Pradesh, and culminating in Leh, Ladakh, the route offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, rugged terrain, pristine lakes, and remote villages.

Experience the thrill of riding on winding mountain roads, crossing icy streams, and camping under the starlit sky amidst the majestic Himalayan peaks.

Trip Highlights

  • Scenic Landscapes: The journey offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, rugged valleys, serene lakes, and pristine glaciers.
  • Challenging Terrain: Riders will encounter diverse terrain ranging from smooth highways to rocky and gravel roads, river crossings, and steep mountain passes.
  • Cultural Experience: Along the way, travelers have the opportunity to visit ancient monasteries, Buddhist stupas, and traditional villages, providing insights into the rich culture and heritage of the region.
  • Wildlife Encounters: The region is home to a variety of wildlife species, including Himalayan marmots, ibex, wild yaks, and rare bird species, offering ample opportunities for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.
  • Adventure and Thrill: Riding through the remote and rugged landscapes of the Himalayas, with ever-changing weather conditions and unpredictable road conditions, makes the trip an exhilarating adventure for motorcycle enthusiasts.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Manali, bike check and orientation

Day 2: Manali to Jispa (Approx. 140km)

Day 3: Jispa to Sarchu (Approx. 90km)

Day 4: Sarchu to Leh via Tanglang La Pass (Approx. 250km)

Day 5-6: Rest and exploration in Leh

Day 7: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass (Approx. 120km)

Day 8: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake (Approx. 150km)

Day 9: Pangong Lake to Leh (Approx. 150km)

Day 10: Departure from Leh
