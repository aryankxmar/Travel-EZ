About Varkala Beach

Varkala Beach, located in the coastal town of Varkala in Kerala, India, is known for its stunning cliffs, pristine sands, and natural spring waters. It is a popular destination for both beach lovers and spiritual seekers, offering a serene and picturesque setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Main Attractions

  • Cliff Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea from the majestic cliffs overlooking Varkala Beach.
  • Mineral Springs: Experience the healing properties of the natural mineral springs believed to have therapeutic benefits.
  • Janardanaswamy Temple: Visit the ancient temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located near the beach, and explore its architectural beauty.

Local Cuisine

Indulge in the flavors of Kerala cuisine during your visit to Varkala Beach:

  • Seafood Specialties: Sample a variety of fresh seafood dishes such as fish curry, prawn masala, and crab roast cooked with authentic Kerala spices.
  • Traditional Sadya: Enjoy a traditional Kerala feast known as Sadya, featuring an array of vegetarian dishes served on a banana leaf.
  • Tropical Fruits: Taste delicious tropical fruits like mangoes, pineapples, and jackfruits, grown abundantly in the region.

Accommodation Options

Discover comfortable stays near Varkala Beach:

  • Cliff Resorts: Stay in luxurious cliffside resorts offering stunning ocean views and modern amenities.
  • Beach Cottages: Opt for cozy beach cottages nestled amidst palm trees, providing a serene and private accommodation option.
  • Budget Guesthouses: Choose from a range of budget-friendly guesthouses and homestays for a comfortable and affordable stay.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Trivandrum, transfer to Varkala Beach

Day 2: Relaxing day at Varkala Beach, sunbathing and swimming

Day 3: Visit Janardanaswamy Temple and explore the local market

Day 4: Excursion to nearby attractions like Kappil Lake and Anjengo Fort

Day 5: Leisure day for Ayurvedic spa treatments and yoga sessions

Day 6: Departure from Varkala Beach, return to Trivandrum
