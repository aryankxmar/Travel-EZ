River Rafting in Rishikesh

About River Rafting

Experience the thrill of white water rafting in the holy city of Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. Rishikesh, renowned as the 'Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas,' offers the perfect setting for adventure enthusiasts to conquer the mighty rapids of the Ganges River.

Our river rafting expeditions cater to all levels of experience, from beginners to seasoned rafters, providing an adrenaline-pumping journey through turbulent waters, scenic gorges, and lush greenery.

Join us for an exhilarating adventure amidst the pristine natural beauty of Rishikesh.

Rafting Highlights

  • Thrilling Rapids: Navigate through a series of exhilarating rapids, including the famous 'Roller Coaster,' 'Double Trouble,' and 'The Wall,' offering an unforgettable rafting experience.
  • Breathtaking Scenery: Immerse yourself in the picturesque landscape of the Ganges River valley, surrounded by verdant forests, towering cliffs, and cascading waterfalls.
  • Professional Guides: Our experienced rafting guides ensure your safety and provide expert instruction, making your rafting adventure both thrilling and memorable.
  • Adventure Packages: Choose from a variety of adventure packages, including half-day, full-day, and overnight rafting trips, tailored to suit your preferences and schedule.
  • Water Activities: Extend your adventure with additional water activities such as cliff jumping, body surfing, and river kayaking, adding to the excitement of your rafting experience.
