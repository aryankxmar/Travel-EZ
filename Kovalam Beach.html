About Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is a stunning coastal paradise located in the southern state of Kerala, India. Famous for its pristine sandy shores, turquoise waters, and picturesque sunsets, it is a favorite destination for travelers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.

Main Attractions

  • Lighthouse Beach: Visit the iconic lighthouse situated on a rocky promontory and enjoy panoramic views of the coastline.
  • Hawa Beach: Relax on the soft sands of Hawa Beach and witness the mesmerizing sight of local fishermen going about their daily routines.
  • Samudra Beach: Explore the tranquil shores of Samudra Beach and indulge in activities like swimming and beach volleyball.

Local Cuisine

Experience the rich flavors of Kerala cuisine during your visit to Kovalam Beach:

  • Seafood Delights: Sample mouthwatering seafood dishes such as fish curry, prawn masala, and crab roast prepared with local spices.
  • Coconut-based Specialties: Savor coconut-based delicacies like appam, puttu, and avial, which are staples of Kerala cuisine.
  • Traditional Sweets: Treat yourself to traditional Kerala sweets like payasam, banana fritters, and unniyappam for a delightful dessert experience.

Accommodation Options

Discover comfortable stays near Kovalam Beach:

  • Beach Resorts: Choose from a range of luxurious beachfront resorts offering stunning ocean views and world-class amenities.
  • Ayurvedic Retreats: Experience holistic wellness at Ayurvedic resorts and rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul with traditional treatments.
  • Guesthouses and Homestays: Stay in cozy guesthouses and homestays run by local families for an authentic Kerala experience.
